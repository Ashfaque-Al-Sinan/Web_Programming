REACHER

A Jack Reacher Thriller

About the Show

Reacher is an American crime thriller streaming television series for Amazon Prime Video. It is based on the Jack Reacher book series by Lee Child.

Producer Information

Produced by: Amazon Studios, Skydance Television, and Paramount Television Studios

Executive Producers: Nick Santora, Don Granger, Christopher McQuarrie, David Ellison, Dana Goldberg, and Marcy Ross.

Summary

The first season follows Jack Reacher's investigation of a counterfeiting operation and a series of murders in the fictional town of Margrave, Georgia. The second season sees Reacher investigating the deaths of members of his former U.S. Army 110th Special Investigations Unit.

Why I Love Reacher

I love Reacher because of its gripping plots, intense action sequences, and the charismatic portrayal of Jack Reacher. The show stays true to the spirit of the books, delivering a thrilling and satisfying experience. Alan Ritchson perfectly embodies the character's physical presence and dry wit. Each season presents a new and intriguing mystery, keeping me hooked from beginning to end.
